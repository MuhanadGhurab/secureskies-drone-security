## C. Sixty-second interview version

SecureSkies was our four-person graduation project at Jeddah International College. We
explored combining perimeter surveillance concepts with network monitoring on a Raspberry Pi
and Pixhawk platform, using Mission Planner and basic monitoring tools, plus cloud Console
concepts. We reached a partially integrated prototype and ran integration tests with twenty
passes, three failures — including gateway, failsafe validation, and compliance — and one
inconclusive machine-learning threat-analysis result. Full autonomous secure deployment was
not completed due to integration, vendor, and schedule constraints. I’m now documenting the
history cleanly and planning a software-only digital twin — without implying production
readiness.
