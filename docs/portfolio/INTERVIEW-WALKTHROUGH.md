# Interview Walkthrough — SecureSkies

Use these grounded questions. Prefer honest “partial / failed / incomplete” answers over
marketing polish.

## Problem and scope

1. What physical and cyber risks was SecureSkies trying to address?
2. Who were the intended operator personas?
3. How did the team separate aspiration from delivered work?

## Architecture and platforms

4. How did Raspberry Pi and Pixhawk roles differ in the architecture?
5. How did Mission Planner fit into operator workflows?
6. What did network monitoring mean in this project?
7. What was known — and unknown — about the AWS Console integration?
8. What does the report mean by “Raspberry Pi API”?

## Hardware

9. Which major airframe and power components were selected?
10. Which hardware claims still need independent verification?

## Testing honesty

11. What testing methodology did the team use?
12. What passed during integration testing?
13. Which three areas failed?
14. Which area was inconclusive?
15. Why can outsiders not fully retest from the public repo?

## Incomplete deployment

16. What does “partially integrated prototype” mean here?
17. Why was full autonomous secure cloud deployment incomplete?

## Team and attribution

18. Who were the four team members and the supervisor?
19. Why does this repo refuse to assign individual technical roles?
20. How should you answer: “Did you personally build SecureSkies?”

Suggested answer for Q20:

> The report verifies Muhanad Ghurab as one of four project team members but does not
> provide a detailed individual responsibility matrix. Specific personal contributions
> require separate verification.

## Lessons and modern path

21. What vendor / sourcing challenges appeared?
22. What project-management lessons would you apply next time?
23. Which modern security controls would you add first in a twin?
24. How do safety and privacy constraints bind a digital twin?
25. How does this project relate to Security+ thinking (CIA, defenses in depth)?
26. How does this project relate to PMP thinking (constraints, risk, stakeholder honesty)?

## Achievement

27. How do you state the Second Place result without overclaiming?

Required wording:

> Second Place — University Graduation Project  
> Owner-verified; supporting artifact pending.
