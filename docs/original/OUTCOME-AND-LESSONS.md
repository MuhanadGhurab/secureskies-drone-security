- Full autonomous secure cloud-connected system **not deployed**

## Completed / advanced work

- Physical assembly reported completed

- Diverse hardware/software integration was underestimated

## Project-management lessons

- Vendor acquisition delays impacted schedule
- Clearer budgeting, resourcing, and specialist skills planning
- Stronger project management discipline under timeline pressure

## Future-work themes (historical aspirations)

Better architecture validation, vendor coordination, testing depth, and talent readiness —
aspirational recommendations, not delivered outcomes.
